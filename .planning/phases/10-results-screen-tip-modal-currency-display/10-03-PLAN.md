Rewrite `PersonResultsScreen` from a single-person breakdown into the locked Results screen: an all-participants accordion (current user expanded by default, others tap-to-expand), a grand "Total" row (items only, D-03), a fixed bottom CTA bar (Copy summary / Edit bill / New Split), and an inline currency-override affordance near the total (D-06/D-07). Other people's cards show item share only — so per-person item shares always reconcile to the grand bill Total.
