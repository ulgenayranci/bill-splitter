## Recurring strategic themes (across all docs)
1. **No-account / link-based is the #1 switching trigger.** Our core bet; lead with it.
2. **OCR accuracy is where incumbents bleed.** Our AI-cleanup layer is the moat — only if it reliably strips non-item lines.
3. **The emotional job is social, not arithmetic** — removing awkwardness/quibbling at the table.
4. **Output a ready-to-send "who owes what" message** to plug into the universal pay-and-reimburse habit.
5. **Drinks/alcohol asymmetry** is the most charged fairness complaint — first-class drink handling differentiates.
